🚀 Getting started

How do I get ModMate AI running in my channel? ▼
Click "Connect with Twitch" on the homepage and authorize ModMate AI. Once connected, the bot joins your channel automatically and starts moderating within seconds. No setup, no code, no configuration files required.
Does the bot need to be a mod in my channel? ▼
Yes — to ban or timeout users, the ModMate AI bot account needs moderator permissions in your channel. Type /mod ModMateAI in your Twitch chat after connecting and it will have the permissions it needs to take action.
Which platforms does ModMate AI support? ▼
Currently ModMate AI supports Twitch. Support for YouTube Live and Kick is on our roadmap — sign up to get notified when those go live.
Can I use ModMate AI on multiple channels? ▼
Each account covers one channel. If you manage multiple channels, you'd need a separate ModMate AI account for each. We're working on a multi-channel plan — reach out if that's something you need.

🛡️ Moderation

What kinds of messages does ModMate AI catch? ▼
ModMate AI detects hate speech and slurs, personal harassment and threats, chat spam and flooding, self-promotion and unsolicited links, sexual or explicit content, and any custom words you add to your blocked list. You can toggle each category on or off from your dashboard.
How does ModMate AI decide to ban vs timeout vs warn? ▼
The AI assesses severity. Bans are for the most serious violations — hate speech, slurs, and severe threats. Timeouts are for spam, flooding, and self-promotion. Warnings are for borderline content or first mild offenses. You can adjust the sensitivity from Lenient to Strict in your settings.
Can I undo a moderation action? ▼
Yes — from your dashboard you can review every action and override it. To unban someone, go to your Twitch channel settings or type /unban username in chat. We're working on one-click undo directly from the ModMate AI dashboard.
The bot banned someone it shouldn't have — what do I do? ▼
False positives can happen — no AI is perfect. To unban them type /unban username in your Twitch chat. To reduce false positives, try switching your sensitivity setting to Lenient in your dashboard. If a specific type of message keeps getting wrongly flagged, reach out and we'll look into it.
Can I add my own blocked words? ▼
Yes — on the Pro plan you can add unlimited custom blocked words from your dashboard. Any message containing those words will be flagged automatically regardless of context.

💳 Billing & plans

What's included in the free plan? ▼
The free plan includes up to 500 moderated messages per day, basic moderation rules (hate speech, spam, NSFW), and 24 hours of chat log history. It's a great way to try ModMate AI before upgrading.
How do I upgrade to Pro? ▼
Go to your dashboard and click "Upgrade to Pro." You'll be taken to a secure Stripe checkout page. Once payment is confirmed your account upgrades instantly with no downtime.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes, cancel any time from your dashboard under Account Settings. Your Pro plan stays active until the end of your current billing period, then switches to the free plan automatically. We don't charge cancellation fees.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We don't offer refunds for partial months. If you have a billing issue or were charged incorrectly, reach out using the form below and we'll sort it out right away.

⚙️ Technical

The bot stopped responding in my chat — what's wrong? ▼
First check that the bot is still a mod in your channel. If it lost mod status it can't take action. Also try disconnecting and reconnecting your Twitch account from the dashboard. If the issue persists, check our status above or contact us below.
How fast does the bot respond to messages? ▼
Typically under 1 second from message sent to action taken. Occasionally AI response times can be slightly longer during high traffic periods, but the vast majority of actions happen fast enough that viewers won't notice.
Does ModMate AI work while I'm offline? ▼
ModMate AI only moderates when your stream is live and your channel has active chat. It does not monitor your channel when you're offline.
Is my chat data stored or sold? ▼
No. Chat messages are processed in real time for moderation and immediately discarded — we don't store message content. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
