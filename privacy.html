Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep it simple and honest — no legal jargon designed to confuse you.

1. Who this applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of ModMate AI — streamers who connect their Twitch account to our service, visitors to our website, and anyone who contacts us directly.

2. What data we collect

We only collect what we need to provide the service. Here's exactly what that is:

Data Why we collect it How long we keep it
Twitch username & account ID To identify your account and connect the bot to your channel Until you delete your account
Email address To send account notifications and billing receipts Until you delete your account
Chat messages Processed in real time to make moderation decisions Not stored — processed and discarded immediately
Moderation action log So you can review what the bot did in your channel 30 days on free plan, 1 year on Pro
Account settings To remember your moderation rules and preferences Until you delete your account
Payment information To process your subscription Handled by Stripe — we never see your card details

3. How we use your data

We use your data only to provide and improve the ModMate AI service. Specifically:

  • To operate the moderation bot in your Twitch channel
  • To send you account and billing notifications
  • To show you your moderation history in the dashboard
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI moderation over time
  • To respond to support requests you send us

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except where necessary to operate the service (see section 5).

4. Chat messages and AI processing

When ModMate AI is active in your channel, every chat message is sent to our AI system for analysis. Here is exactly how that works:

  • Each message is sent to the Anthropic API (the AI that powers ModMate AI) for analysis
  • The AI returns a moderation decision — safe, warn, timeout, or ban
  • The message content is not stored by us after this analysis is complete
  • Only the moderation action taken is logged, not the full message text

Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. You can read it at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Third parties we work with

We use a small number of trusted third party services to operate ModMate AI:

  • Twitch — to connect to your channel and execute moderation actions
  • Anthropic — to power the AI moderation engine
  • Stripe — to process subscription payments securely
  • Netlify — to host the ModMate AI website

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and data practices. We choose partners who take privacy seriously.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — only what is necessary to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools.

7. Your rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Access all data we hold about you by contacting us
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect ModMate AI from your Twitch channel at any time
  • Export your moderation log from your dashboard

To exercise any of these rights, use the contact form below or disconnect your account from the dashboard settings page.

8. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure storage of credentials, and limited access to user data within our team. However no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we ever become aware of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly.

9. Children's privacy

ModMate AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do we will update the date at the top of this page and notify active users by email for any significant changes. Continued use of ModMate AI after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
